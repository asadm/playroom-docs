# No gameId provided in insertCoin

You called `insertCoin()` without a `gameId`. This will impact analytics and enforce DAU limits soon.

```
insertCoin() called without gameId. This may impact analytics and enforce DAU limits soon.
Fix it here: https://docs.joinplayroom.com/errors/no-game-id
```

To fix it, create a project on the [Playroom Dev Portal](https://dev.joinplayroom.com) and paste your `gameId` into `insertCoin`.

## How to get your Game ID

<Steps>

### Go to the Dev Portal

Start by navigating to the [Playroom Dev Portal](https://dev.joinplayroom.com/signin). This is where you'll manage all your game projects and get your unique `gameId`. You'll see the **Dev mode** sign-in form ready for you to get started.

### Enter your email

Type your email address in the **Dev mode** field and click **Continue**. This will send you a verification link to confirm your identity.

![Playroom Dev Portal — enter your email](/images/portal/1-enter-email.png)

### Check your email

You'll receive a **Sign in to Playroom** email from `noreply@joinplayroom.com`. Open the email and click the **Sign in to Playroom** link. This authenticates you and logs you into the Dev Portal.

### Choose a plan

After logging in, you'll need to select a plan to activate your account. The **Hobby** plan is completely free and perfect for testing and development. Select the plan that works best for your needs.

![Playroom pricing — choose a plan](/images/portal/2-choose-plan.png)

### Click + New Project

Once your plan is activated, you'll land on the dashboard. Here you can see all your projects. Click the **+ New Project** button in the top right corner to create a new project and get your `gameId`.

![Playroom dashboard — click + New Project](/images/portal/3-click-new-project.png)

### Name your project

A dialog will appear asking you to name your project. Enter a project name using only letters and numbers, then click **Continue**. This name helps you organize and identify your projects.

![Create project dialog — enter project name](/images/portal/4-name-project.png)

### Open your project

Your project is now created! You'll see it appear in the left sidebar under **Applications**. Click on your project name to open it, then click the **Configuration** tab. This is where you'll find your `gameId`.

![Project overview — open Configuration tab](/images/portal/5-open-project.png)

You'll land on the Configuration page. This page contains everything you need—your `gameId` in the **General** section at the top, and the **API key** section below. You can see the full layout here:

![Configuration page overview — gameId and API key](/images/portal/8-configuration-page.png)

### Generate your Game ID

On the **Configuration** page, scroll down to the **API key** section. This is where your `gameId` gets generated. Click **Reset API Key** (or **Create** if this is your first key). The system will generate a unique key for authenticating your game.

![Configuration page — API key section](/images/portal/6-generate-api-key.png)

### Copy your Game ID

After generating the API key, scroll back up to the **General** section at the top of the Configuration page. You'll see your `gameId` field is now populated with your unique ID. You have two options:

- **Option 1:** Copy just the `gameId` value from the field
- **Option 2:** Copy the ready-made `insertCoin()` code snippet shown in the yellow notification box—it includes your gameId and is ready to paste into your code

![Configuration page — copy your gameId](/images/portal/7-copy-game-id.png)

</Steps>

## Use the Game ID in your code

Paste the `gameId` into your `insertCoin` call:

```js
insertCoin({ gameId: "your_game_id_here" });
```

<Callout type="info">
  The `gameId` is read-only and assigned when you create a project. You can always find it again in your project's **Configuration → General** tab.
</Callout>
