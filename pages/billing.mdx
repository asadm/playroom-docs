### Custom

### How we charge

We charge based on **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** — unique users who opened or played your game from a new device in the past month. There are no hidden fees for server uptime or bandwidth.

Your bill is calculated at the end of each month based on your actual usage.

### Credits
- If your account has credits, they will be charged first before your account gets billed
